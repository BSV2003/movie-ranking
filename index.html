The Best Movies According to Sri Vaishnavi Balina

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Businessman

Businessman is a gripping and intense tale of a young man's journey from being a small-time street-smart hustler to becoming a powerful and influential underworld don.

KGF

The story of KGF showcases the rise of a simple man from the slums who eventually becomes a powerful and feared mafia leader in the gold mining town.

Twilight

Twilight is a captivating tale of forbidden love between a vampire and a human, exploring the complexities of loyalty, sacrifice, and the power of true love.
